# Theme: Super Mario

- **id**: mario
- **vibe**: a classic NES side-scrolling platformer level
- **layout**: banner (wide, own row)
- **palette**: sky #5C94FC, ground tan #E0843? , pipe green, brick orange, Mario red cap/shirt + blue overalls

## States
- **working**: a seamless scrolling level — Mario runs rightward and **hops** over a green pipe, then
  **hops** a goomba; a mushroom sprouts once per loop; coins, brick + `?` blocks, clouds and a hill
  scroll past. ~3–4.5s loop.
- **waitingPermission**: Mario stopped, looking up at a flashing `?` block — "your move".
- **waitingTurnEnd**: Mario idle with a "zZ".
- **finished**: Mario at the flagpole + castle, "CLEAR".
- **failed**: black screen, "GAME OVER".
- **idle**: a small dim Mario standing on darkened ground.

## Sounds
- **start**: a coin blip.
- **waiting**: a soft two-tone pause beep.
- **success**: a bright ascending victory arpeggio.
- **fail**: a descending game-over tune ending low.
(All synthesized chiptune — no game audio.)

## Token bands (requires agent-island 0.4.0+)
The `working` animation powers Mario up as the session burns tokens:
- **rookie** (< 50k): small classic Mario.
- **super** (< 100k): powered-up, sparkles.
- **fire** (< 200k): white overalls + a fireball, more coins.
- **star** (≥ 200k): flashing invincible colours + sparkles + the most coins.

Only `working` changes per band; the other states are the same across tiers.
